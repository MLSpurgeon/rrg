The Red River Gorge

Kentucky

Advanced GIS: Red River Gorge

The red river gorge is a registered national landmark, located inside the Daniel Boone national forest. It is a beautiful, active park with a natural land bridge. The gorge is rumored to hold the treasure of John Swift's lost silver mine. There are other wonderful treasures here that need to be protected including three endangered species. The Indiana bat, the Virginia big-eared bat and the Red cockaded woodpecker.
